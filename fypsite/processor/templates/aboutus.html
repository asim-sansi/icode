About

iCode is a small and new startup, based in the capital city of Pakistan, Islamabad. We started off in the year 2019 after a group of friends came up with mutual development ideas and decided to encode them to reality. Thus they sat down on a table and joined their heads until they finally agreed to work on a considerably ambitious project. A year later, iCode was born, a company, and also a tool. It revolutionised the IT Industry, specifically the Web Development Sector. It's prospects were limitless and helped iCode grow to what it is today. We now operate as a small but strong team, working towards building, shaping and designing a brighter and more automated future!
